Case Study: Jobbyist

Empowering African Talent for the Global Stage

Project Overview

Jobbyist is an in-house platform developed by Monogamy Digital for Gravitas Industries, LLC, a US-based company. As a dynamic, pan-African career platform, Jobbyist is designed to connect top African talent with global job opportunities—and vice versa—within a single, unified platform at little to no cost.

Our Approach

Inclusive & Scalable Design

The platform is engineered for accessibility and broad adoption. Built as a lightweight, mobile-first web application, Jobbyist ensures maximum usability even in areas with limited connectivity or low-spec devices.

Ad-Supported for Accessibility

To remove cost barriers, Jobbyist is supported primarily through advertising. This model allows job seekers across all socio-economic backgrounds to access opportunities without subscription fees or hidden costs.

Pan-African Vision, Global Standards

From the outset, Jobbyist was designed to serve all African countries while adhering to global best practices in platform design, scalability, and multilingual support.

Launch and Roadmap

Upcoming Launch

Jobbyist is set to launch on July 1st, 2025. It will initially go live in key African markets with a phased expansion across the continent throughout the year.
